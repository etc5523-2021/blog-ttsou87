Introduction

Data Description

Two data sets are used in this analysis.

COVID-19 Confirmed cases and Death

variable class Date
date date Date in Year-Month-Day format.
newCasesBySpecimenDate integer Number of people with at least one positive COVID-19 test result on the day.
cumCasesbySpecimenDate integer Accumulated number of people with at least one positive COVID-19 test result.
newDeaths28DaysByDeathDate integer Number of deaths of people who had had a positive test result for COVID-19 and died within 28 days of the first positive test.
cumDeaths28DaysByDeathDate integer Accumulated deaths of people.

Data Limitations

Vaccination in the United Kingdom

variable class description
date date Date in Year-Month-Day format.
cumVaccinationFirstDoseUptakeByPublishDatePercentage double Cumulative percentage of population age 16 and above with the first dose of vaccine
cumVaccinationSecondDoseUptakeByPublishDatePercentage double Cumulative percentage of population age 16 and above with the two doses of vaccine
